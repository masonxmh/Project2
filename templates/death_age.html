Age and Sex Factors vs. Population died from Coronavirus


In terms of two lines, the number of female patients increased from the age of 30, and the death peak was between 61 and 70. However, the number of male patients increases from the age of 30, and 60 to 90 years are all the peak death age.The biggest gap is between 60 and 90. There are several possible reasons for the disparity in data. It may be the difference between men's physical health, living habits, and hobbies with women. it may also be related to the fact that there are far fewer women than men in the cases that are officially disclosed with specific information.

Overall, the risk of dying from the new coronavirus was higher in older adults than in younger children, regardless of gender. Thirty-five were over 60, with a median age of 70. But the youngest is only 36. Older people have a chronic medical history that makes them particularly cuiruo exposed to the virus
